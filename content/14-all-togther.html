A few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the sun and is the largest of the terrestrial planets. The earth is the only planet in our solar system not to be named after a Greek or Roman deity. It is the only known planet to support life.

About the earth

  • Diameter at equator: 12,756 km
  • Satellites: The Moon

Top 3 earth facts

  2. A year isn't exactly 365 days, and the earth doesn't take 24 hours to rotate
  3. 70% of the earth's surface is water
